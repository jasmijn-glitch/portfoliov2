About me

Young and energetic. Full of life and always ready to expand.

I’m Jasmijn Schenk, A digital designer with a passion for anything creative.

I started with Graphic Design, at Grafish Lyceum in Utrecht, where I specialized in Illustration. Right now, I’m working on my bachelors degree at Hogeschool van Amsterdam. I study Communication and Multimedia Design, and have ambitions to become a UX & UI designer.

I love creation, it’s something I could do every day without getting bored. Projects like: Web design, Application design, Prototyping, Illustration, Graphic design and sometimes even modern day art are my passion. I could work on these projects day and night, because doing what you love will never feel like working.
