Posters DIYR 2021

I designed posters for the yearly festival of the Dutch International Youth regatta that would take place in 2021. The target audience is youth within the ages of 15 and 19 years old, so the posters should be playful and lively.

The Dutch International Youth Regatta is organized to give all Dutch junior rowers a chance in one place to experience their favorite sport together. A junior competition based on a well-known foreign example: Munich, Bremen and of course Ghent. The DIYR aims to enable all rowers under the age of 19, from the Netherlands and surrounding countries, to sail fun and competitive competitions at their own level.
